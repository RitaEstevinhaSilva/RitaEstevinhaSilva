About

Born in Figueira da Foz, Portugal.

I am an aspiring communication designer who has a multidisciplinary background. My bachelor’s degree in Design from the University of Lisbon allowed me to work within the most varied design fields. I believe I am able to solve complex problems from the design realm with an holistic approach. Even though I am in love with the whole design subject, graphic design is my passion. Working previously as a prepress technician provided me fundamental knowledge on printing production and materials, as well as enhanced my interest in graphic possibilities.

Currently, I am doing a master’s degree on Design and Multimedia from the University of Coimbra, hoping to develop some tech skills. So far, I was able to develop this beautiful website. Thanks to a big amount of patience, self-love and YouTube tutorials.

I also like good humour, pink (the colour), reading, writing, travelling, technology (qb.), deep conversations and philosophical themes. I am an insatiable curious.
